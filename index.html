Static Data Visualizations

Goal Distribution by Match Phase and Team

This D3 bubble chart vividly displays the distribution of goals across different match phases in the Qatar 2022 World Cup, categorized by continent. Each bubble represents a team, with its size proportional to the number of goals scored during a specific phase of the gameâ€”Early Game, Mid-Game First Half, Mid-Game Second Half, and Late Game. The colors of the bubbles are strategically used to indicate the continent to which each team belongs, enhancing the visual differentiation and making it easy to identify geographical patterns in playing styles or strategies. This visualization not only serves as a tool for exploring how different teams perform throughout various stages of a match but also highlights the influence of continental football cultures on team performance.

Interactive Data Visualizations

Distribution of Non-Penalty Goals

Explore the distribution of non-penalty goals across match minutes. This visualization allows you to interact with the data to see more detailed statistics for selected ranges.

Shot Map

This interactive shot map provides insights into the quality and location of shots taken during the match. It shows the expected goals (xG) value for each shot, indicating the probability that the shot will result in a goal.
